Day 1: Chest, Shoulders & Triceps

Chest

  • Flat Barbell Bench Press (Mid Chest) - 4 sets x 8–12 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press (Upper Chest) - 4 sets x 8–12 reps
  • Decline Bench Press (Lower Chest) - 4 sets x 8–12 reps
  • Chest Flys (Mid Chest) - 4 sets x 10–15 reps

Shoulders

  • Overhead Press (Barbell or Dumbbell) - 4 sets x 8–12 reps
  • Lateral Raises (Lateral Head) - 4 sets x 10–15 reps
  • Rear Delt Flys (Rear Deltoid) - 4 sets x 10–15 reps
  • Arnold Press (All Heads) - 4 sets x 8–12 reps

Triceps

  • Close-Grip Bench Press (Mid/Long Head) - 4 sets x 8–12 reps
  • Overhead Tricep Extension (Long Head) - 4 sets x 10–12 reps
  • Tricep Dips (Mid Head) - 4 sets x 8–10 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns (Short Head) - 4 sets x 10–15 reps
